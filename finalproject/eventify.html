Eventify

Eventify is a representative name for an event planning website, conveying the idea of simplifying event planning and management.

Site Purpose

The site provides a comprehensive event planning solution, offering a user-friendly interface for creating and managing event details, including dates, times, locations, and guest lists.

Scenarios

  • What are the essential steps to plan a successful event?
  • How do I manage my guest list and RSVPs?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #3498db (used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #f1c40f (used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

  • Open Sans (used for headings and body text)
  • Montserrat (used for titles and emphasis)
